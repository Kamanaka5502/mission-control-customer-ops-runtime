# Customer Discovery Template

Use this during forward-deployed intake to convert ambiguous customer needs into an executable operational workflow.

## Customer problem

What workflow currently creates operational risk, delay, ambiguity, or manual coordination overhead?

## Protected effect

What action should not happen unless conditions still hold?

## Authority

Who or what may authorize this action?

## Evidence

What evidence must exist and remain fresh?

## Approval model

Who reviews, approves, escalates, or rejects the request?

## Failure modes

What would make the action unsafe, stale, out of scope, or invalid before execution?

## System integrations

Which tools, APIs, databases, or customer systems need to be connected?

## Success metric

What does the customer need to see to trust the system?
